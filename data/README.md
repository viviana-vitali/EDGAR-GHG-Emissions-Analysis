# Data

The analysis uses data from the [Emissions Database for Global Atmospheric Research (EDGAR)](https://edgar.jrc.ec.europa.eu/), developed by the European Commission Joint Research Centre.

Furthermore, the data on income groups used for Chart 2 is sourced from the [World Bank Country and Lending Groups](https://datahelpdesk.worldbank.org/knowledgebase/articles/906519-world-bank-country-and-lending-groups), ensuring consistent and reliable measurements of global greenhouse gas emissions.
